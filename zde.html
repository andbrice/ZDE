Basic Controls

Game only works on PC

Moving camera: WSAD or up down left right

Switching between lanes: Left or Right + SHIFT

Play for Victory

Objective:

Build towers in first lane to defend against enemy packets. Send packets in second lane to defeat enemy systems

Improving your towers and packets

Upgrading:

Packets will become available depending on the player's level of energy. These can be upgraded, by clicking on the button located beside the packet name. Towers can also be upgraded by selecting one and choosing an upgrade.
