This is a homepage of a game named Zero Day Exploit. The game was made in JAMK 2015 in Game Project course by Team TDF.


This tower defense game introduces the attack and defense on both sides while managing a strategy among the systems as you fight to maintain control.
The hackers find their way of life under attack from an unknown force, and their recent activities have sparked outrage in the community. A vote for attack and identification of this enemy has been called and the challenge of doing so has been taken up by the hacker elite.

Zero Day Exploit (ZDE); a common hacking term that refers to taking advantage of security vulnerabilities on the same day that these become generally known; thus, there are zero days between the time the vulnerability is discovered and the first attack (Rouse, 2010).
