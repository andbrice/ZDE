ZDE - Zero Day Exploit

Basic controls
Game only works on computers.

Moving camera: WSAD or up, down, left, right arrows
Switching between lanes: Left or Right SHIFT

Play for victory.
Objective

Build towers in first lane to defend against enemy packets. Send packets in second lane to defeat enemy systems

Making your towers and packets better.
Upgrading

Packets are available when you have enough energy.
You can upgrade your packets from a button beside packet name.
You can upgrade placed towers by clicking one and choosing an upgrade.
